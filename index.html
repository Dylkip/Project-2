WHAT WE DO

Friends of Texas Wildlife is a group of trained and permitted volunteers who provide care for injured, displaced and orphaned wildlife in Montgomery and surrounding Counties. We support the rehabilitation of native Texas wildlife and promote co-existence of wildlife and people through education.

REHABILITATE

Many of the animals Friends of Texas Wildlife receives require immediate veterinary care to survive. Rehabbers provide care 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

EDUCATE

In addition to caring for wildlife, Friends of Texas Wildlife offers educational resources to prepare you in the case that you find an abandoned or injured animal.
